Sequoia National Park

Location

Sequoia National Park is adjacent to Kings Canyon National Park in California's southern Sierra Nevada mountains. Check out the map Below!

Map

Hiking

There are more than 1,000 miles of Sequoia & Kings Canyon hiking trails! Top-rated Sequoia hikes include Big Trees Trail, Crescent Meadow Loop Trail, Congress Trail, Tokopah Falls Trail, Hazelwood Nature Trail, Alta Peak Trail, and the High Sierra Trail to Bearpaw Meadow.

Where to stay

Cabins and camp sites! Make sure to reserve your spot!

Reservations

Points of Interest

Sequoia is known for its huge sequoia trees, notably the General Sherman Tree dominating the Giant Forest. The underground Crystal Cave features streams and striking rock formations.
